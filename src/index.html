Hotel Diva - A Chic Union Square Hotel

Hotel Diva, a San Francisco Hotel, is a true gem among luxury Union Square Hotels.

Sexy, modern and absolutely not for everyone, Hotel Diva is the most chic hotel in Union Square and all of San Francisco. Conveniently located on Geary Street just 2 blocks away from top San Francisco Shopping venues like Neiman Marcus and Saks, Hotel Diva has a style that is attractive and architecturally appealing.

Upon arrival, stroll past the one and only Diva 'Sidewalk of Fame', featuring signatures of celebrity guests and into the modern yet, sophisticated lobby of our Union Square Hotel.  Sexy custom shades and stainless steel sculpted headboards set the stage in your room, for your own Diva experience.

With an excellent location near the shops and San Francisco Restaurants of Union Square, guests can find everything right outside the door of our Union Square Hotel. Hotel Diva is also close to the best Things to do in San Francisco. Walk directly across the street and find two of the city's best performing arts theaters, The American Conservatory Theater (ACT) and the Curran Theatre. Our location is perfect for those who want to explore all the best the city has to offer. 

Both business and leisure travelers appreciate the thoughtful amenities at our Union Square Hotel such as a Business Center, Concierge service, and much more. Guests can take advantage of Colibri Mexican Bistro and Starbucks both located just off our lobby, 24-hour fitness center and newly remodeled business center.

At Hotel Diva, every guest is a VIP. Don't settle for an ordinary San Francisco Hotel. Choose Hotel Diva and see just how extraordinary San Francisco can be!

Visiting San Francisco?  Choose Hotel Diva - A San Francisco Hotel, located in the heart of beautiful San Francisco and close to many San Francisco Things to do and more. Check Rates, Make Reservations call: 415.885.0200.

  • “Funky Boutique Hotel in Union Square”

    Only stayed here one night, but it's a great hotel with high-end shopping and good eats right nearby. The rooms are quite big. We had a bedroom, bathroom and a separate room with desk and cafe table. This extra room also has a murphy bed so comfortably fits two friends with each having some privacy. I was here for fun this trip but for the business traveler it's perfect. While the hotel does not have an expansive lobby where you can meet customers, it does have private little lounges you can use. They are perfect meeting spots for two to four people. They are big enough to be comfortable but small enough that no one else will want to share and crash your meeting.

  • “Take me back!”

    Great location, walking distance to everything. Thanks to the concierge for assisting my partner with arranging the gorgeous flowers when i checked in so early in the morning :) loved every minute of this amazing city cannot wait to get back.

  • “Great Place!”

    My husband and I visited for a two night stay in San Francisco. The location was fantastic and the staff was very welcoming. Upon check in, the manager asked what brought us to town and we mentioned that we were traveling on our "babymoon". She then sent up Ghirardelli chocolates, a bottle of wine for my husband and a cute book about pregnancy. It was so thoughtful and a fantastic way to start our trip. The bed is super comfortable...we slept really well. The room and bathroom are small but had everything we needed. We would stay here again!

  • “Hotel Diva for a Diva”

    I was a little worried about how modern this would be for our mini family weekend but it was great! My parents loved the hotel and the entire staff was attentive and so pleasant! The location of the hotel is great as well.. close to all transportation, restaurants and union square! The hotel its self was awesome.. I used the gym and it had pretty much had everything i needed to work out with as well as a TV, Towels etc.. cute gym. I'll always try to make it to this hotel in the future for sure-

  • “Nice and good location!”

    The hotel is very well located. The room we stayed was clean, well decorated but a little dark. We were on the third floor and was a little noise. What was missing in the hotel was a good restaurant for a breakfast. But with several dinners options in the neighborhood.

  • “Very chic & funky”

    Stayed Feb 2014, on advice from Travel Agent, good location to City centre. Boutique style hotel with funky decor. Rooms were clean and not large but ok, service was great. Outside door entry on footpath, check out prior hotel celebrity customers who have stayed at hotel and scribed in wet concrete their signatures and thank you to hotel comments. Would recommend

  • “Great place to stay”

    I have been wanting to stay here for some time, and finally found a good deal on the hotels website. The junior suite had plenty of room and was comfortable. Location is right across from the Curran Theater, 1 1/2 blocks from Union Square. Staff was super friendly, place was clean, and we had an all-around nice weekend in SF. I will absolutely consider the Diva in the future.
